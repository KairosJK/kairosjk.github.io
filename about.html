About


    Hey, I’m Jon, and this is my small blog website. I’m currently a first-year software engineering student, but find enjoyment in many other fields, especially mathematics, history, politics, and philosophy. The goal of this site is just to be able to have a place to post some of my essays I have felt proud of, or a place to post updates to stuff I’m working on and thinking about. Recommendations are always appreciated, either send me a message over one of the accounts on /socials or over my email.

How is the site organized?


     I plan to have the site organized into two major post types. Those being posts and essays. Posts essentially being writeups that I do semi-frequently, which involve some thoughts that have been around in my head or some political subject that has been floating around in the public conscious for a while. Essays are more focused and research-driven work. I expect most of them will be about history or film, just because I find it a lot easier to write and research about them.
